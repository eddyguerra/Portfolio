Discovery

Understanding the Problem


Audience

Individuals who are figuring out how to get into a specific career/ industry as quickly as possible. Those users will usually be between the ages of 18-35.


Need

A way of gaining easy access to industry veterans who will be able to impart knowledge and give actionable advice to the user.


Opportunity

To help the users connect with a community of like minded individuals and senior level career personnel, this will provide them with tools that will expedite their path to gaining a successful career.

User Research

Competitor Analysis

My first order of business was to understand my competitors—how they were being received by users, and what they had to offer them.

After some browsing through the Apple and Google Play stores, it became apparent that while there was not much career guidance apps but their was clear favorites that dominated the field.



Profile of top performing app- Pathsource


Strenghts

  • Easy to use interface
  • Notifications on what is going on in the career field

Opportunities

  • Add more blogs and professionals in the field to talk to users
  • Professionals and users could have a social/recruiting platform

Weakness

  • Lack of modern careers in the system
  • UI could be improved upon

Threats

  • Apps with more modern information on careers and new in demand careers
